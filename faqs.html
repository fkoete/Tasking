Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Simply contact our support team, and we'll assist you with the process.

What is the refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first 14 days, you can request a full refund.

How do I get started with your services?

Getting started is easy! Simply browse our services, choose a plan that fits your needs, and contact us to schedule a consultation. We'll guide you through the rest.

What types of tasks can a virtual assistant handle?

Our virtual assistants are highly versatile and can handle a wide range of tasks, including administrative support, data entry, customer service, social media management, scheduling, and more. We also offer specialized industry-specific virtual assistants.

How do you ensure the security and confidentiality of my data?

Maintaining client security and confidentiality is of utmost importance to us. We have stringent security measures in place, including vetted employees/service providers and transparent business practices.

Are your pricing plans flexible?

Yes, we offer flexible pricing plans to suit various needs and budgets. We have daily, weekly, and monthly options, as well as customized pricing for specific requirements.
