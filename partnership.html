Partnership Model Overview

Partnership Model Overview Investment/Commission Structure Benefits
Service Providers For individuals, students and businesses delivering virtual assistant or elderly care support services. Tasker247 is committed to addressing social needs by creating employment opportunities and offering flexible work arrangements. Commission-based model with three different commission percentages (deducted from service fees) based on the service provided.
  • Access to a consistent referral network
  • Flexible service delivery with your existing infrastructure
  • Ongoing training and support in a rewarding field that makes a positive impact on society
Jurisdictional Partners For businesses or sole proprietors aiming to serve an entire province. Our two-tiered pricing model adapts to the size of the province, ensuring equitable access to our national brand and support. Two-Tiered Pricing:
  • Smaller Provinces: Nominal initiation fee and monthly support fee
  • Larger Provinces: Adjusted initiation fee and monthly support fee based on regional size
  • Exclusive territorial rights to maximize your market potential
  • Full access to our partner portal for uniform operations and national branding
  • Regular training and dedicated operational support
  • Leverage our brand’s social impact to drive community growth
